Elon's Achievements
  • SpaceX (Founder/CEO): Revolutionized space exploration by developing the Falcon 1, 9, and Heavy rockets and the Dragon spacecraft. Achieved the first commercial launch, orbit, and recovery of a spacecraft, and the first private company to send astronauts to the International Space Station.
  • Tesla, Inc. (CEO/Product Architect): Led the popularization of electric vehicles (EVs) through models like the Roadster, S, 3, X, and Y. Transformed EVs from niche products to mainstream, and expanded into solar energy and battery storage.
  • X (formerly Twitter) (Owner):> Acquired Twitter in 2022 for $44 billion, implementing significant changes to its operations and content policies.
